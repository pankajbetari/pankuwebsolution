Global Web Technologies LLP.

Founded 15 years ago and headquartered in the Safdarjung Development Area, New Delhi, Global Web Technologies LLP has established itself as a leading provider of digital solutions in India. Our company specializes in delivering turnkey web development projects, custom web applications, and innovative digital experiences for mid-size to large-scale enterprises.

At Global Web Technologies, we pride ourselves on our expertise in a wide array of technologies such as PHP, Laravel, Bootstrap, jQuery, MySQL, and more, ensuring robust, scalable, and future-ready web applications. Over the years, we have successfully completed over 800 projects, ranging from corporate websites and e-commerce platforms to complex enterprise applications, making us a trusted partner for clients in various industries.
